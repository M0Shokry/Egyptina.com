Hurghada

hurghada is the administrative capital of the Red Sea Governorate, occupying 40 km along
the western coastline of the Red Sea. It is bordered by: North: Ras Gharib. South: Safaga.
East: Red Sea Coast. West: Red Sea Mountains.

Giftun Island:

Giftun Island is the first nature reserve on the Red Sea coast and the most important in the
tourism sector. It holds a special status as the only island where visitors are allowed to
disembark, dive, and enjoy the colorful coral reefs and diverse species of fish. Even if you do
not dive, you can still admire the underwater beauty due to the island’s crystal-clear waters.
Additionally, Giftun Island is a significant natural habitat for seagulls, hosting half of the
seagull species found in the region.

Top Activities on Giftun Island:

Rent a boat and disembark on the island’s pristine sandy beach, untouched by human
development. Take a boat trip into the open Red Sea for diving or swimming, where you can
marvel at the vibrant fish and coral reefs. Explore the island and enjoy birdwatching,
particularly seagulls, along with other bird species and reptiles.

Hurghada Grand Aquarium (Aquarium Museum)

• The Most Important Museums in Hurghada Opened in 2015, the Hurghada Grand
Aquarium offers a stunning panoramic representation of marine life. The exhibits
are divided into four main sections:

1. Freshwater Fish Section
2. Saltwater Fish Section
3. Wadi Al-Hitan Section (Valley of the Whales)
4. Touch Pond Section

Top Activities at the Aquarium

Explore the museum and discover its diverse marine and terrestrial wildlife. Walk through
the 24-meter-long underwater tunnel for a unique viewing experience. Observe the large
marine tank, which houses aquatic life in a controlled environment. Visit the freshwater
tank, home to various aquatic species preserved for research purposes. Let children enjoy
the dedicated play area with fun activities. Take a break in the Bedouin tent inside the
museum and enjoy some traditional drinks. Experience a tropical rainforest zone, which
shelters different wild animals. Relax at the museum’s café, where you can enjoy light
snacks, hot or cold drinks, and desserts.

The Sand Museum

The sand museum is one of the most famous museums worldwide, as it uniquely utilizes yellow
and white sand. It is the first of its kind in Egypt and the Middle East, despite the concept
being well-known globally. The museum houses 42 sand sculptures, crafted by 42 renowned
sculptors from around the world, depicting historical figures, modern icons, and beloved
cartoon characters.

Top Activities at the Museum:

• Stroll through the museum and admire the impressive sand sculptures, including
cartoon characters like Spider-Man, Batman, and Captain America.
• Marvel at historical figures such as Cleopatra, Napoleon Bonaparte, the Sphinx, and
replicas of the Pyramids.
• Discover famous Disney characters, brought to life through intricate sand art.
• Enjoy the on-site restaurants, kids’ amusement areas, and rest zones, ensuring a fun
and relaxing experience for visitors.
