New Alamein City

New Alamein City is one of Egypt’s most significant urban development projects, located
along the country’s northern coast. The city aims to balance economic growth with
environmental sustainability, serving as a model for smart and sustainable cities in the
region..

• Geographical Location

•New Alamein City is situated in the Matrouh Governorate, covering an area of over 48,000
acres. It is bordered to the north by the Mediterranean Sea, making it an ideal destination for
coastal tourism.

Vision & Objectives

The city is designed to provide a fully integrated environment for both residents and visitors, offering essential facilities and services such as hospitals, schools, and universities, along with entertainment and commercial zones.

Development Phases

• Phase One: Included the development of basic infrastructure and the construction of
public facilities such as schools and hospitals.

• Phase Two: Focused on developing residential and commercial areas, along with parks
and recreational zones.

• Phase Three: Centered on completing urban projects and expanding industrial zones.
